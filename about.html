OUR STORY

For over 9 years, JO n Jud8 Salon has been in the business of making clients look and feel good. In 2007, JO n Jud8 joined in a hair and make-up competition in Hair Asia and Ricky Reyes Salon and had won multiple awards, medals, plaques, and trophies.

We dedicate ourselves to your individual needs. From runway to everyday, we at JO n Jud8 Salon desires to create your personal style that takes every facet of your needs into consideration and infuse creative, customized creations that are most suitable for your desires. We are not just any "beauty shop", we are dedicated to be your personal Salon Guru.

Our mission is to provide exemplary services through a passion for styling and fashion, helping our guests to live beautifully, head to toe. Put yourself in the hands of beauty experts who bring the creative spark of passionate artists, and offer the integrity of experienced professionals.
